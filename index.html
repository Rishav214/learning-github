(emphasis) Paragraphs are the building blocks of papers. Length and appearance do not determine whether a section in a paper is a paragraph. For instance,(italics) in some styles of writing, particularly journalistic styles, a paragraph can be just one sentence long. Ultimately, a paragraph is a sentence or group of sentences that support one main idea.
